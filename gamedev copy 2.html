Call of Duty®: Black Ops 6

Associate Game Designer II - Multiplayer

As part of Treyarch's core multiplayer gameplay team, I work on high-impact features and systems under NDA.

This is my first AAA title. I joined Treyarch as an intern during the summer of 2021. Within the first month, I had a feature greenlit.
I've since returned to work at Treyarch full-time beginning in 2022 to work on Black Ops 6.

Please look out for more info on my work once Black Ops 6 releases October 25th.

Skylost (PC) (2022)

Lead Designer

As Lead Designer on Skylost, I managed a team of 3 designers (level, gameplay/systems, and UI) among a student team of over 30.

Together, worked diligently for a full-year, and we shipped a 3D, open-world, action-adventure game which became a featured selection of the 2022 USC Games Expo.



What started as a project where I thought my primary focus would be to continue sharpening my own designs, quickly turned into valuable lessons on management.
